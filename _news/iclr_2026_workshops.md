We had three different workshop papers accepted to ICLR 2026!

Check out our work:

* On few-step models of conditional SDEs, [Stochastic Few-step Models](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=i31AUZF8kO), accepted at both the [DeLTa](https://openreview.net/forum?id=nmczKNW73P) and [ReALM-GEN](https://openreview.net/forum?id=i31AUZF8kO) workshops,

* on reversible ODE/SDE solvers for flow/diffusion models, [Rex](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2502.08834), accepted at both the [DeLTa](https://openreview.net/forum?id=rHIUKCqPBZ) and [ReALM-GEN](https://openreview.net/forum?id=8sw868HPho) workshops, and

* on using LoRA as security patches for large vision systems, [Elytra](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.00661), accepted at the [Trustworthy AI](https://openreview.net/forum?id=mmM7CVfzVw) workshop.
